Behavior 1

Closes on Scroll

A scroll event listener on window immediately closes any open dropdown the moment the page begins to scroll. Open a menu, then scroll down to see it vanish.

Behavior 2

Auto-Close Timer

After opening, the dropdown runs a 4-second countdown. The shrinking progress bar visualises the timer. If not interacted with, it removes itself from the DOM automatically.
