    how to keep audio playing with screen off

    browsers and youtube actively limit background playback. here's what actually works:

    • picture-in-picture first — right-click the video and choose "picture-in-picture" (desktop). on android chrome, tap the small icon in the youtube player. PIP almost always survives screen lock.
    • android: install this page as an app (browser menu → "add to home screen" or "install app"). standalone PWA mode usually keeps audio playing.
    • ios: when locked, audio may pause — tap play from the lock-screen controls to resume. the media session is registered so the controls appear.
    • keep the tab in the foreground if PIP is unavailable. switching apps will likely pause playback.

    the loop button has three states: off → all (loop the queue) → one (repeat the current track). everything in your queue is saved automatically.
